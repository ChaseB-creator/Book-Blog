Survival Novel

By Gary Paulsen

The Hatchet

Hatchet is a 1987 young-adult wilderness survival novel written by American author Gary Paulsen. It is the first novel of five in the Hatchet series. Thirteen-year-old Brian Robeson is on a plane trip to visit his father in the oil fields of northern Canada when the pilot suffers a heart attack and dies mid-flight. Brian crash-lands the plane into a lake in the Canadian wilderness. With only a hatchet, he must learn to survive alone in the forest.
